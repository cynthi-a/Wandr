About Wandr

This page is dedicated to all the romantic, curious and thrill-seeking adventurers out there! With Wandr, we provide a platform that allows you to share your best holiday snaps with like-minded globetrotters. Our mission is to let the community inspire each other and trigger a chain reaction that promotes wandrlust. We help people around the world to connect and inspirie each other by sharing their dream destinations.
